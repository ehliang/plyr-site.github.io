About

Our Mission

The Prime Leaders of York Region will take initiative in raising awareness within the community concerning local, provincial, federal, and global social issues, educating and encouraging the public to take action.

Our Vision

Our efforts will work toward realizing a more informed, involved and inspired community. In our efforts, we shall always respect and promote fundamental values such as respect, equality, compassion, collaboration and leadership.
